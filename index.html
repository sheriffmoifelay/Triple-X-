ABOUT US

ADJOSEC INVESTMENT LIMITED




Commonly Known as TRIPLE X

ADJOSEC INVESTMENT LIMITED is Buduburam's first branded Hotel since 1994 and the only exclusive modernized hotel in Kasoa-Buduburam which offers over 50 well furnished rooms, ranging from Queen-sized rooms with all neccessaties to single unit rooms with simple appliances. At this Hotel, you have a backup plan for welcoming your visitors who arrive in an emergency or upon informing you. Just click the call button on this page to speak to our customer service agengy or click the Whatsapp button to be directed to have a chat or video communication with us. Feel free in doing business with ADJOSEC.

Founded by Mr. Kwabena Owusu and also the current C.E.O, it has been one of the (if not the ) best companies in the surrounding, offering job opportunities for over 50 employees, mainly the youths.
