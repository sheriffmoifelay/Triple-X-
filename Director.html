BIOGRAPHY

Mr. Kwabena Owusu born February 2nd, 1968 in Kumasi , nothern Ghana. He is a business man and an investor who is focused driven on improving lives of people mostly in need. While growing up in the slum of Agbobloshie Market of Ghana, he hustled to make life. So, whenever he comes across someone from such background or in a challenging stories, he always wants to be a help as he strongly believes his life began at the ghetto .
He is the husband to Mrs. Dorcas Boakye, since ----, who is also his business partner running ADJOSEC INVESTMENT LIMITED.

One of his companies is ADJOSEC, which is based in Buduburam, in Gomoa-East District Central Ghana.
